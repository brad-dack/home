AML/CTF Program Setup (Parts A & B)
From $499

Designing a practical, documented AML program tailored to how your agency actually operates.

Part A – Framework
  • Review of regulatory guidance and AML risk considerations.
  • Assessment of your business model and operational structure.
  • Identification of key risks across customers, transactions and geography.
  • Development of a documented AML risk assessment and risk appetite.
  • Drafting or co‑designing your AML Program Part A (policies, procedures, controls).
Part B – CDD
  • Design of practical customer identification and verification processes.
  • CDD procedures that work with your existing workflows.
  • Guidance on verification options: manual, pay‑per‑use tools, low‑cost services, or full platforms.
  • Documentation of your AML Program Part B framework.

Delivered collaboratively so your program is usable day‑to‑day, not a binder that sits on a shelf.

Health Check (recommended bi‑annual)
From $599 + $10 / file

A review to confirm your program is in place and working as intended.

What we review
  • Existing AML documentation and program content.
  • Evidence of training and awareness across your team.
  • Sampling of onboarding and verification files (15% sample).
What you receive
  • Operational testing of key processes.
  • Practical recommendations prioritised by risk and effort.
  • A concise report you can table with your principals or governing body.

Designed to be repeated every 6–12 months to keep your program on track.

AUSTRAC Annual Report Support
From $499*

Support to prepare and lodge your Annual Compliance Report and governing body report on time.

How we help
  • Pre‑report planning, key dates and AUSTRAC Online setup checks.
  • Review of preview questions and information requirements.
  • Collation of SMRs, TTRs, CBMs, internal escalations and training records.
  • Testing outcomes across onboarding, verification and reporting.
Deliverables
  • Drafted responses for the Annual Compliance Report (ACR).
  • Signed Annual Report to the Governing Body (Sections 1–12).
  • Narrative on effectiveness, deficiencies and recommendations.
  • Record‑keeping pack stored in line with the 7‑year requirement.

*Discounted when bundled with a Health Check for the relevant period.

Independent Review
$1,799 + $30 / file

A deeper, independent review of your AML program, operations and evidence.

Scope
  • Review of AML/CTF Program Parts A & B.
  • Training compliance and competency checks.
  • Operational testing of key processes (30% sample).
Outcome
  • Detailed findings and remediation recommendations.
  • Report suitable for your governing body and AUSTRAC enquiries.
  • Optional follow‑up support to implement changes.

Independent Reviews are required every 2–3 years by an entity not involved in the setup or maintenance of your program.
